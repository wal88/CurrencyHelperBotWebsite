About the Currency ChatBot

The Currency ChatBot was designed to meet all bank customer needs for all things foreign exchange related.

Besides finding the latest exchange rates and converting amounts effortlessly, the ChatBot has several other useful functions such as management of currency reserves and converting prices in an image.

This will prove invaluable for bank customers due it's ease of use using this responsive website or the facebook page messenger version.

This proof of concept has a lot of potential and new features can be added to make it even more useful.

Exchange Rates and Conversion

Get live exchange rates just by asking me. I can retrieve exchange rates for any two currencies, or specify the base currency and a list of currencies to get multiple quotes. I can also convert amounts from one currency to another.

Management of Currencies held

You can create an account to store all currencies you hold, just ask me to add, delete or view currencies in your account. I can also calculate the total value of your currencies into any currency you want using current exchange rates.

Conversion of prices in an image

You can send me an image with prices on it, and I will convert all prices from the source currency to a currency of your choice. I will them send you back the image with the new converted prices!
